About Us

Our Story

The Iron Moka Pot

Our origin and symbol. Our Moka pot has been passed down in our founders family for generations so when you enjoy one of our coffees you are tasting a rich family history steeped into the iron.

Our Mission

To honour the art of traditional brewing while offering a warm, welcoming space for our community. Honest coffee crafted with care.

Our Values

  • Craftsmanship - Every cup is brewed with intention.
  • Authenticity - Honest flavours and honest methods.
  • Community - A café built for connection, we have a personal library and several board games.
  • Sustainability - Ethically sourced beans, responsible practices.

What Makes Us Different

  • We brew using moka-inspired methods rooted in our original iron pot.
  • We specialise in premium Blue Mountain coffee.
  • Our café blends vintage industrial charm with warm, modern comfort.
  • Every drink is handcrafted, roasted in our own personal roastery - nothing rushed, nothing mass‑produced.

Our Vision

As we grow, we remain committed to the heart of Iron Brew — the iron pot that started it all. We’re expanding our roasting craft and looking to branch out into our own unique blends by partenering with local farmers and distributors.
